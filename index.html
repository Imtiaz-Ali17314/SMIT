📖 My Educational Journey

👤 Imtiaz Ali — From Gilgit-Baltistan

🌱 Early Education

I began my education in my native village Phakar, studying from Nursery to 8th grade at the well-known Uswa Public School.

At that time, students from Jamia Urwa-tul-Wusqa, Lahore used to visit villages during summer vacations and run classes and workshops for children — creating a spiritual, academic, and worshipful environment.

🕌 Journey Towards Madrsa

Together with students of Jamia Urwa-tul-Wusqa I travelled to Lahore. In Rawalpindi I know about Jamia Jafria Gujranwala (a branch). Impressed by its system, I joined there.

Daily Schedule at Jamia Jafria

  • ⏰ Wake-up: 30 minutes before Fajr
  • 🕌 Prayer: Immediately after Adhan
  • 🏃 Exercise → 🍽️ Breakfast → 🎤 Assembly
  • 📚 Classes until Zuhr
  • 🍴 Lunch → 😴 Rest (2–3 hours)
  • 📖 Study till Maghrib
  • 🍽️ Dinner → 💬 Discussions till 10 PM
  • 🌙 Rest for the night

I studied there for 2 years and completed Matriculation with 77% marks (2nd position in Bio-Science, Jamia Jafria 10thclass). As Jamia Jafria offered education up to Matric only, I was sent to Jamia Urwat-ul-Wusqa, Lahore for higher studies.

🎓 Higher Education

At Jamia Urwa-tul-Wusqa, Lahore I continued religious studies for 5 years and earned the Almiyah Degree (equivalent to M.A).

Simultaneously, I completed Intermediate from the Lahore Board in May 2021, achieving 1036/1100 marks (1st Position in the Jamia). 🏆🌟

💻 Introduction to IT

An IT expert joined the Jamia as Head of IT and launched IT courses. Although I initially hesitated, I quickly enrolled once I learned the courses were computer-related.

Having loved computers since childhood (I even bought my first PC during school), I had served as In-charge of the Computer Lab at Jamia Jafria. After passing a selection test, I joined the Modern Web Application Development course and completed it while continuing religious studies.

💼 Professional Development

I worked about 1.5 years in Jamia's IT department where I learned Networking, Hardware, and Web Development. I also completed online courses in Freelancing, WordPress Development, and Graphic Designing.

After moving to Karachi, I reconnected with my trainer who brought me into his development company. I completed internships and fast-tracked courses in Laravel, Vue.js, React.js, and Node.js, then started working on live projects.

I also joined the Saylani Mass IT Training (SMIT) program in Modern Web Application Development after passing a competitive test and am continuing my studies at the SMIT head office in Bahadurabad, Karachi.

📢 Teaching & Preaching Experience

To develop my preaching and community skills, I served for two years at Darul Uloom Hujjatiya, Gilgit (a branch of Jamia Urwat-ul-Wusqa). During this time my web development work paused, but my interest in software development remained strong.

🌟 Future Vision

  • ✅ Complete Modern Web Application Development training
  • ✅ Enhance software development skills
  • ✅ Build a strong IT career
  • ✅ Teach and uplift others by sharing knowledge

🙏 I pray to Allah to bless me with success and the ability to contribute positively to society.
